DHEA: Bringing Art Together in the Digital World

What We're About:

DHEA is a group of artists working together in the digital world. We believe in the power of art and want to make sure it isn't lost in the online world. Our goal is to build a community where artists can support each other, share their work, and create amazing projects together.

Addressing Recent Issues:

We've had some disagreements in DHEA lately about individual freedom within the group. We want to be clear: DHEA is about bringing unique voices together, not forcing everyone to be the same. We want everyone to feel comfortable expressing themselves.

Here's what we're doing right away:

  • Open Discussion: We'll have a virtual meeting where everyone can share their concerns, ideas, and hopes for DHEA.
  • Clear Agreement: We'll write a document together that outlines our shared values, how we'll communicate, make decisions, and work together. This will also explain the difference between personal projects and DHEA collaborations, making it clear that joining DHEA projects is always a choice.

Our Plan:

Phase 1: Building Our Foundation (September 7th - September 21st, 2024)

  • Define Our Vision: We'll create a guide that explains DHEA's artistic style and ideas. This isn't a rulebook, but a reference for those who want to join our projects.
  • Build Our Website (doll.network): We'll design our website to include artist profiles, a blog for discussions, a portfolio of our work, and a section dedicated to DHEA's story and world.
  • Find Like-Minded Artists: We'll invite artists who share our vision to join DHEA. We'll be upfront that joining projects is voluntary and highlight the benefits of showcasing both individual and collaborative work on our website.
  • Welcome New Members: We'll create a welcoming process for new members, introducing them to DHEA's values, guidelines, and upcoming projects.
  • Brainstorm Project Ideas: We'll hold a brainstorming session with all members to come up with creative project ideas that combine music, visuals, and maybe even interactive elements for our website.
  • Choose Our First Projects: We'll choose a few top project ideas and clearly define their goals, deliverables, roles, timelines, and a flexible structure for collaboration.

Phase 2: Sharing Our Work (September 22nd - October 20th, 2024)

  • Launch Our Projects: We'll kick off our chosen projects, ensuring everyone is on the same page and contributes in their own way.
  • Develop Our Website: We'll finish building doll.network, making it user-friendly and visually appealing, with a focus on individual artist pages and a constantly growing world and story.
  • Share Our Thoughts: We'll use our website to share blog posts, artist statements, and behind-the-scenes looks at our individual and collaborative work.
  • Spread the Word: We'll use social media to connect with others, share our creative process, and build a community that values both individual expression and collective effort.
  • Create Merchandise: We'll design limited-edition merchandise that represents DHEA's spirit, allowing fans to show their support for the collective and individual artists.

Looking Ahead:

DHEA is constantly evolving. We believe in open communication, mutual respect, and valuing everyone's creativity. By combining individual expression and collaboration, we aim to create lasting art in the digital world, proving that art driven by passion will always have a place.
